CV

As a final-year Computer Engineering student with Honors in Machine Learning and Data Science from MKSSS Cummins College of Engineering for Women, Pune, I have developed a strong foundation in technology. I gained hands-on experience through internships at Deutsche Bank, where I deployed three critical microservices in a DevOps role, earning a quarterly award for being the first intern to achieve this recognition. At Reliance Jio, I contributed as a FrontEnd Intern, focusing on the design and implementation of their employee portal. Currently, I am working on transfer learning models with a focus on the attention module, aiming to create a custom model for cancer classification as part of my research at the CMATER Lab, Jadavpur University, under the guidance of Prof. Ram Sarkar.
